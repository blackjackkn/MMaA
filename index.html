About Us

Asphalt is meant to last for decades. Then again, we live in Colorado! Our weather extremes, heavy traffic and life in general all conspire to do a number on asphalt.
Mountain Maintenance & Asphalt works on all manner of residential, commercial, industrial and municipal asphalt projects. For asphalt seal coating, line striping, patching and cleaning in the Vail, CO area, no one tops the Mountain!

Experienced & Locally-Owned Business

When your asphalt needs servicing, you're giving technicians access to your property. You deserve to have a company who is reliable and reputable on the job. Not only are we locally-owned with years of experience, we are licensed, bonded and insured. Call today for an estimate on your asphalt service needs.

Services & Maintenance

We offer seal-coating, line striping, and power washing. If cracks do appear, we'll patch them up in a jiffy using high-tech equipment and know-how.

Range of Service

From our home base in Edwards, CO, we can travel virtually anywhere in Eagle County. Super store in Silverthorne? We will be there. Gym in Gypsum? Just say the word. Look for our trucks out and about in Vail, Gypsum, Frisco, Avon, Dillon, Glenwood Springs and anywhere else within a 50-mile radius of these communities. Don't see your town on the list? Give us a call!

Accredited Business

Mountain Maintenance & Asphalt are licensed and insured, and are accredited by the Better Business Bureau.
